## Message text (default version)

The default message is short and warm. It is modeled on the wording proposed at [Exchange #22 Round 4, Move 5](../agent/exchanges/entry-trust-domain-expert-practitioner-exchange.md). Edit lightly for voice, but preserve the four structural moves it carries.

> Hey — I took your feedback seriously. I rewrote one specific thing as a one-pager about how childcare licensing actually flows in a real city, not as a theory. If you ever feel like skimming it and telling me what I got wrong about your experience, that would be useful. If not, we are good either way and I will not keep nudging.

The four structural moves to preserve when adapting voice:

1. **Acknowledgment that her prior feedback drove the change** — not "I made another thing," but "you said X, so I tried Y."
2. **A bounded, specific ask anchored in her domain** — "skim a one-pager about childcare licensing in a real city," not "review my work" or "give me feedback on the project."
3. **An honest framing of what the artifact is** — "a one-pager," "in a real city, not as a theory." No oversell.
4. **An explicit out** — "if not, we are good either way and I will not keep nudging." This must be in the message itself, not implicit.
